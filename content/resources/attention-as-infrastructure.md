# Attention as Infrastructure

This resource explores practical patterns for shaping environments where deep concentration can happen by default.

## Why it matters

When work is fragmented, creativity and strategic reasoning collapse.

## First protocol

Create one recurring block each week where meetings are forbidden and synthesis is mandatory.
